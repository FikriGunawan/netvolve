About Us

Founded in 2009, Netvolve offers Information and Communications Technology (ICT) services for various businesses from different industries. Part of Jala Lintas Media Group, we pride ourselves as a trustworthy, reliable, and customer-oriented Internet Service Provider and Network Access Point for both end users and businesses, offering value-added services in the form of one-stop and end-to-end managed ICT solutions covering all areas in Indonesia.
