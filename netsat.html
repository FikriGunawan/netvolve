NetSat - VSAT

NetSat is a business solution that uses a very-small-aperture terminal (VSAT) media, allowing it to reach every part of Indonesian Territory. Netvolve offers Ku-Band and C-Band NetSat services.

VSAT - SCPC

VSAT â€“ SCPC is a telecommunication service with SCPC (Single Channel Per-Carrier) technology that uses Point-to-Point and Point-to-Multipoint topology with transfer speed rate of up to 40 Mbps. It also acts as a Private Dedicated Bandwidth Service for data, sound or video applications and can be used on your own device. It offers dedicated bandwidth for those who need continuous communication with great bits to areas outside the reach of the network. This service also provides Data, Video and Image Communication Services.
